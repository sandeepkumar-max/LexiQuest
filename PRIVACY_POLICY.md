# Privacy Policy for LexiQuest

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

LexiQuest ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application, LexiQuest (the "App"). This policy is designed to meet Google Play Store's Data Safety requirements.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

- **Account Information**: When you sign up or log in using Firebase Authentication (Google, Email, etc.), we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
- **Payment Information**: We use **Razorpay** to process payments for premium subscriptions. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers; they are handled securely by Razorpay.

### 1.2 Usage and Technical Data

- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your mobile device, including the model and operating system version.
- **Android Advertising ID**: We collect the **Android Advertising ID** to provide personalized advertisements through **Google AdMob**. This ID is used for ad targeting and analytics.
- **App Activity**: We track your progress, including learned words, test scores, and streaks, to provide a personalized learning experience.

## 2. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data.

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between the App and our backend (Firebase) is protected using **SSL/TLS encryption**.
- **Storage**: Your personal and app data is stored in Google Firebase's secure cloud infrastructure.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the App's features (Vocabulary building, Tests, etc.).
- To manage your account and subscription status.
- To personalize your experience (Daily Words, Missions).
- To send you daily reminders (via Local Notifications).
- To display advertisements (via AdMob).

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

- **Google Firebase**: For authentication and cloud storage (Firestore). [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Google AdMob**: For displaying advertisements. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Razorpay**: For secure payment processing. [Razorpay Privacy Policy](https://razorpay.com/privacy/)

## 5. Account Deletion and Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

- **How to Delete Your Account**: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
  - **In-App**: Via the "Delete Account" option in the **Settings/Profile** menu.
  - **Email**: By contacting us at **sandeepkumarspj290@gmail.com**.
- Once a deletion request is processed, your data will be permanently removed from our active databases.

## 6. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: To send you daily study reminders.
- **Camera/Photos**: To allow you to upload a profile picture.
- **Internet**: To sync your data and fetch dictionary entries.

## 7. Children's Privacy

LexiQuest is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
